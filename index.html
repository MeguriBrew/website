What is Suspended Coffee?

Imagine walking into your favorite café and ordering a coffee. Before you leave, you pay for an extra cup that stays for someone else to claim later, whenever they need a little cheering up.

At MeguriBrew, we bring this century-old tradition into modern life, making it easy to share a warm cup of kindness wherever you are — at your local café or even from afar.

  • Born in Naples, Italy

    where customers would pay for an extra coffee for someone less fortunate

  • Pay it forward

    kindness in its quietest form: anonymous and rooted in community

  • Send a ripple

    simple gesture that spreads warmth and human connection, one cup at a time

From the Founder

Follow my journey
Elina Aizenberg
Founder, MeguriBrew
After moving to Tokyo in 2024, I fell in love with its rhythm and found peace and safety there. I’m deeply grateful for all of it, and I wanted to give something back.

MeguriBrew grew from that wish — to offer a simple way for people to feel connected, even in the rush of city life.
And I believe a small act of kindness, like sharing a cup of coffee with someone who needs it, can start a ripple.

That’s what MeguriBrew is about: creating those gentle ripples, one cup at a time.

How It Works

Simple steps in MeguriBrew app for Customers and Cafes to spread joy and connect with your community through the universal language of coffee.
For Customers
Step 1 - Choose a café
Browse the map or list to find a nearby café. Tap it to see its drinks and choose whether to share or claim a cup.
Step 2 - Share or claim a drink
To share, select a payment method and pick a drink. To claim, choose an available drink and show your QR code or 4-digit code to the café staff.
Step 3 - Send or receive kindness
Leave a kind note when you share or respond with ‘Thank you!’ if someone made your day with a cup.
For Cafés
Step 1 - Set up your account
Add your preferred payment options, choose which drinks to offer as suspended, and set the prices. You can update these anytime.
Step 2 - Confirm a claim
When someone wants to claim a suspended drink, ask for their QR code or 4-digit code — then confirm and serve.
Step 3 - Register payments
If a customer wants to pay for a suspended drink in the café, accept the payment and confirm their QR or 4-digit code.
