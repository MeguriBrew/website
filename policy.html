Privacy Policy
Effective date: 23 June 2025
At MeguriBrew, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.
This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

  1. Information We Collect
We collect the following personal information:
  • Email address – when you join our waitlist.
  • Name, email address, and message content – when you submit a message through our contact form. This information is not stored but is forwarded directly to our corporate email.
  • Payment-related information – when you choose to support the project. We do not collect or store your payment details. All transactions are processed securely by Stripe, a third-party payment processor.

2. How We Use Your Information
We use your information only for the following purposes:
  • To notify you about updates related to MeguriBrew (waitlist emails).
  • To respond to inquiries you send via the contact form.
  • To process voluntary payments securely through Stripe.

3. Sharing of Information
We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except in the following cases:
  • Stripe: for secure payment processing. You can read Stripe’s privacy policy here.
  • Email service providers: used to deliver messages you send via our contact form.

4. Cookies and Tracking
We do not use cookies or other tracking technologies on this website.

5. Data Security
We take reasonable steps to protect the personal data we handle. Messages submitted through the contact form are transmitted via encrypted protocols, and we rely on trusted third-party services (like Stripe and email providers) to handle sensitive data securely.

6. Your Rights
Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
  • Access the personal data we have about you
  • Request deletion of your data (such as from our waitlist)
To make a request, please contact us at: info@meguribrew.com

7. Third-Party Services
This website uses third-party services including:
  • Stripe – to process payments. Stripe is responsible for handling financial data in accordance with their own privacy practices.
  • Email Provider – to receive contact form messages.

8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users by updating the "Effective date" at the top of this page.

9. Contact
If you have any questions about this policy, please contact:
Email: info@meguribrew.com
Owner: MeguriBrew LLC
Website: www.meguribrew.com
